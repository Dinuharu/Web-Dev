Classic ramen

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredient Quantity
Chicken broth 4 cups
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Miso paste 1 tbsp
Ramen noodles 2 packs
Soft-boiled eggs 2
Sliced pork belly 4 pieces
Green onions 2 stalks, chopped
Nori (seaweed) 2 sheets
Sesame oil 1 tbsp
  1. Heat chicken broth in a pot and stir in soy sauce, miso paste, and sesame oil.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Arrange the cooked noodles in bowls and pour the hot broth over them.
  4. Top with sliced pork belly, soft-boiled eggs, chopped green onions, and nori.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious ramen!
